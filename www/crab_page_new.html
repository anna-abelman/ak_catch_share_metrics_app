BSAI Crab

Bering Sea and Aleutian Islands Crab Rationalization Program

Implemented

2005

Quota species

Bering Sea and Aleutian Islands red king crab, golden king crab, blue king crab, Tanner bairdi crab, and Tanner opilio (snow) crab

Management features

Harvester (vessel owner and crew) and processor allocations, community protection measures

Baseline years for catch share metrics

Average of 1998/99, 2001/02, and 2004/05 fishing seasons
